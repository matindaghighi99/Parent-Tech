The shared foundation

The platform every venture stands on.

The unglamorous, decisive layer: sovereign compute, a security baseline, compliance scaffolding, and a cleared bench — built once, inherited by all.

Platform layers

01

Sovereign cloud

Canadian-resident compute (Taiga) sized for regulated and classified workloads. Every venture deploys here by default — residency is never an afterthought.

02

Security baseline

A hardened, audited starting posture every company inherits: identity, logging, threat detection, and incident response, ready before the first line of product.

03

Compliance & clearance

Privacy and threat-risk assessment templates, data-sharing agreements, and personnel clearance pathways — the gates that stop most vendors, cleared once for everyone.

04

Talent & capital

A pooled bench of cleared engineers and operators, and patient capital to fund the pilots and reviews that decide whether a venture lives.

05

Shared research

Models, evaluation harnesses, and safety tooling developed once and reused — so each venture starts from the frontier, not from scratch.
